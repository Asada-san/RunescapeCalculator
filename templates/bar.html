Revolution Bar

Calculate the Average Ability Damage Per Tick (AADPT) as a percentage of the base damage of a revolution bar by simply dragging and dropping abilities onto the bar below followed by pressing the "FIGHT!" button.

Upon the press a fight between a player and a dummy is simulated. If so desired, it is also possible to see more information by clicking on the resulting DPT number (the script output is cut off after 3 min). Even more information can be seen by checking the "Print more info" option under Python Script.

Clicking on an ability links to the ability's RS3 wiki page.

  • For every situation, average ability damages are used
  • Tuska's Wrath does its normal damage only. When fighting slayer creatures, please don't let revolution activate this ability and instead activate it manually once every 2 minutes
  • As the name A --> Ability <-- DPT suggests, auto attacks are not taken into account
  • All ability information used to determine the AADPT can be viewed here
  • All ability images were yoinked from the RS3 wiki
